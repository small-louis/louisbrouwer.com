About

I build physical systems. My background is mechanical engineering (Edinburgh, First Class, IMechE Best Student Award) and design innovation (Imperial College London and the Royal College of Art).

Right now I work as an R&D engineer at Rivan Industries, a climate tech startup developing direct air capture and synthetic fuel technology. I designed and validated the 100kW carbonation prototype, one of three core modules in the DAC system architecture.

Before engineering I ran a furniture business called Boog Design out of my parents' garage. Over 25 commissions delivered, roughly £50k in revenue, starting at age 15. I still make things for myself and people I care about.

I've captained a 57 foot sailing yacht across the Atlantic and back. Four crossings total. I surf whenever I can.

I hold UK and Dutch citizenship.
