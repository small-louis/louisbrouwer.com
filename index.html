About

I studied mechanical engineering at Edinburgh (First Class, IMechE Best Student Award) and I'm finishing a masters in Innovation Design Engineering at Imperial College London and the Royal College of Art.

Right now I work as an R&D engineer at Rivan Industries, a climate tech startup building direct air capture and synthetic fuel technology. I designed and tested the 100kW carbonation prototype there.

Before university I ran a furniture business called Boog Design out of my parents' garage. I started it at 15 and delivered over 25 commissions for roughly £50k in total revenue. I still build things by hand when I can.

I've captained a 57 foot sailing yacht across the Atlantic and back. Four crossings total. I surf whenever the waves are good.

I hold UK and Dutch citizenship.

Kinetic Canvas

IDE Cyberphysical Systems / 2025

A kinetic sculpture that moves fabric through mechanical linkages and servo motors. I designed the mechanical system, wrote embedded control software with the team, and built the brand. It won the Interplay competition and we pitched it commercially at PS Labs in Bermondsey.

Mechanical Engineering Electronics Software Branding

Rivan Industries: DAC Carbonation System

Rivan Industries / 2025 to Present

I designed and tested a 100kW carbonation prototype for a direct air capture system. The work covers the full loop from research through prototyping and system modelling.

Mechanical Engineering Thermodynamics Prototyping

LeveL: Gambling Harm Prevention

IDE Group Project / 2025

A gambling harm prevention system that uses vibrotactile stimulation, open banking, and location awareness to intervene in real time. I ran a double blind controlled trial and built the measurement hardware and the Swift app.

Service Design Scientific Method Software (Swift) Hardware

SuperGreen: Roman Concrete Stool

IDE SuperGreen Module / 2025

A 75kg stool cast from Roman concrete (cocciopesto), a lime and crushed brick mix that heals itself in the rain. The form borrows from classical Roman furniture and is designed for outdoor use.

Material Science Physical Making Sustainability
